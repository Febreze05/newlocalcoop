
Game title & 1–2 sentence pitch:
- Glidebreakers 
– It’s air hockey turned up to 11. Move around the table, hit the puck, and gain game changing power ups. Fast and chaotic just like the real thing.


Player count & supported devices:
- Player Count: 2 players 
- Devices: PC (Windows)

Controls (per player):

- Controller:
Can be player 1 or 2 
Left Stick – Glide paddle

-Keyboard:

If a controller is plugged in:

Keyboard player always uses WASD

If two players are on keyboard:

Player 1 – WASD

Player 2 – Arrow Keys
